Our Story

  1. 1990

    Zoo Planet founded as a small rescue centre focused on native wildlife.

  2. 2005

    Expanded with new habitats and public education programs.

  3. 2015

    Launched conservation partnerships and community outreach.

  4. 2023

    Major renovation of exhibits and launch of our digital learning portal.
