We're committed to providing an inclusive experience for all visitors

Wheelchair Accessible

All pathways, exhibits, and facilities are wheelchair accessible. Wheelchairs available for rent at entrance.

  • Smooth, paved pathways
  • Accessible restrooms

Sensory-Friendly Options

Quiet areas and sensory-friendly hours available for visitors with sensory sensitivities.

  • Quiet zones throughout zoo
  • Sensory maps available
  • Early morning quiet hours
  • Noise-canceling headphones

Visual Accessibility

Services and accommodations for visitors with visual impairments.

  • Braille signage
  • Audio descriptions
  • Service animals welcome
  • Tactile exhibits

  • ASL interpreters (by request)

  • Visual alert systems
  • Written tour materials
