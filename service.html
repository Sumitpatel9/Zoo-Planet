Accessibility Services

Inclusive Experience

Wheelchair Access

All main paths and facilities are wheelchair accessible with ramps and elevators available.

Hearing Assistance

Sign language interpreters available upon request and hearing loops at all shows.

Visual Assistance

Tactile maps, braille guides, and audio descriptions available at the entrance.

Sensory-Friendly Hours

Every Wednesday morning (9-11 AM) with reduced crowds, sounds, and lighting.

Mobility Equipment

Wheelchairs and ECVs available for rent on a first-come, first-served basis.

Special Assistance

Our staff is trained to provide any additional assistance you may require.
