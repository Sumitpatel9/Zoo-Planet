Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

We're open Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and weekends from 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM. During holidays, we operate from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Please check our website for any special closures.

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend booking tickets online in advance, especially during peak seasons and weekends. This ensures entry and helps us manage visitor capacity for the best experience.

Yes! All our facilities are fully wheelchair accessible. We also offer complimentary wheelchair rentals at the main entrance (first-come, first-served basis). Service animals are always welcome.

Outside food and non-alcoholic beverages are permitted in designated picnic areas. We also have several restaurants and cafes on-site offering a variety of dining options. Glass containers and alcoholic beverages are not allowed.

Absolutely! We offer educational programs for all ages, including guided tours, school field trips, summer camps, and workshops. Contact our Education Department at education@zooplanet.com for more information and bookings.

Tickets can be cancelled or rescheduled up to 24 hours before your visit date for a full refund. For same-day cancellations due to emergencies, please contact our support team who will review on a case-by-case basis.
