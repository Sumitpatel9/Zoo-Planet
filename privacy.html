1. Introduction

Zoo Planet (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and protect your information when you visit our website, make bookings, or interact with our services.

2. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information automatically collected when you use our services.

Information you provide

  • Contact details — name, email, phone, postal address;
  • Booking information — visit date, ticket type, payment details (payment processors handle card data);
  • Membership and donation information;
  • Volunteering applications, resumes and other application materials;
  • Support requests and communications with our team.

Automatically-collected information

  • Device and browser information (IP address, user agent, device type);
  • Usage data — pages visited, actions taken, referral URLs;
  • Analytics and cookies (see Cookies section).

3. How We Use Your Data

We use personal data to:

  • Process bookings, payments, memberships and donations;
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries;
  • Send transactional communications (booking confirmations, receipts);
  • Send marketing communications where you have opted in (you can unsubscribe anytime);
  • Improve our site, services and personalise your experience;
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience and gather analytics. Cookies may be:

  • Essential: Required for core functions (bookings, security).
  • Performance: Aggregate analytics to improve site performance.
  • Functional: Preferences and personalization.
  • Advertising: Third-party advertising and tracking (only where permitted).

You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings. For detailed list of cookies we use, contact us or view the cookie policy (if available).

5. Third-Party Services

We may share data with trusted third parties who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processors (Stripe/PayPal), email providers, analytics (Google Analytics) and hosting providers. These parties process data only as necessary and under contract.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights such as:

  • Access to personal data we hold about you;
  • Correction of inaccurate data;
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations);
  • Object to or restrict processing; and
  • Data portability requests.

To exercise rights, contact us using the details below. We will respond in line with applicable law.

7. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical and physical safeguards to protect personal data. However, no system is 100% secure. If a breach occurs, we will follow applicable notification obligations.

8. Children

Our services are not directed at children under 13 (or local age of consent). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, contact us to request removal.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will post the updated policy here with a revised effective date. Continued use after updates indicates acceptance of the changes.
